About

About me

I'm a third-year Cognitive Systems student at UBC, specializing in Brain and Cognition. Currently working as an AI Developer at the Department of National Defence and as a UI/UX Designer at UBC Startups Club.

I build real-world apps that solve real problems — whether that's helping UBC students find people to eat with, or helping seniors preserve their memories through voice. AI is what I'm most obsessed with right now, and I'm always looking for ways to make it actually useful for people.

Outside of tech, I'm at the gym most days, always chasing a new trail to run, trying new restaurants, cooking high-protein meals, and reading whenever I can. Trilingual (French, English, Mandarin), and happiest when I'm doing too many things at once.
